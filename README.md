# Meet the Arctic

What do we know about Artic zone? Do we know any cities name? Or anything about severe conditions there? This project will introduce you some Acrtic human settlements, places where people surviving their lives, even if you can't say that from the first look.


It's little portal with themed postcards. They are absolutely free for use under license [CC BY-ND](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nd/4.0/legalcode).
